Getting Here

Let us help you plan your trip.
Driving

Port Macquarie is less than 5 hours drive from Sydney with plenty of lovely costal towns to visit along the way.

Flying

Flights from Melbourne, Sydney and Brisbane can be found for great prices with the Port Macquarie Airport only a 15 minute drive from the center of town.

Train

The NSW Country Link train stops at Wauchope, a lovely country town about a 20 minute drive from the Port Macquarie city center. From the station you can easily catch a bus or taxi to Port Macquarie.
