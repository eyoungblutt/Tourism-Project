Things To Do

Find the perfect activity for you and the family!

There are so many amazing things to do in Port Macquarie that it's impossible to lise them all. Enjoy incredible coastal or rainforest walks, fine dining and theatre, a day at the zoo or, of course, a sun soaked day on any number of our incredible ebachers. We've selected just a few of our favourites to get you started.

Beaches, beaches and more beaches! There's a new one for every day of the week.
Sea Acres National Park is a beautiful day out away from the city center. Strole the boardwalk or enjoy a meal at the Sea Acres Rainforest Centre, all whils surrounded by the peaceful rainsorest.
The Billabong Zoo is a must for all animal lovers. Reknowned for its Koala encounters, the zoo also boasts an array of exocitc and native animal, with encounters, talks and tours avaiable throughout the parlk.
Make a trip to the beach even more memorable by enjoying the view from atop a camel. You haven't seen Port Macquaire until your experienced this longstanding tradition.
For those who like fine dining we recommend Cassegrain Winery. Just a short drive out of town the winery offers delicous meals, cellar door access and a hedge maze for those braze enough to venture in.
Why not grab an ice cream and go for a walk along the Town Beach Breakwall. Tourists and locals alike have been painting images and messages along the breakwall for years. Maybe you'll see something you recognise, or even leave a message of your own?
