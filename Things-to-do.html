Beaches, beaches and more beaches! There's a new one for every day of the week.

Sea Acres National Park is a beautiful day out away from the city center. Strole the boardwalk or enjoy a meal at the Sea Acres Rainforest Centre, all whilst surrounded by the peaceful rainsorest.

The Billabong Zoo is a must for all animal lovers. Re-knowned for its Koala encounters, the zoo also boasts an array of exocitc and native animal, with talks and tours available throughout the park.

Make a trip to the beach even more memorable by enjoying the view from atop a camel! You haven't seen Port Macquaire until you've experienced this longstanding tradition.

For those who like fine dining we recommend Cassegrain Winery. Just a short drive out of town the winery offers delicous meals, cellar door access and a hedge maze for those braze enough to venture in.

Why not grab an ice cream and go for a walk along the Town Beach Breakwall. Tourists and locals alike have been painting images and messages along the breakwall for years. Maybe you'll see something you recognise, or even leave a message of your own?
